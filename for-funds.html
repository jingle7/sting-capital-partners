For Funds

Built for managers raising real capital.

We work with funds across the alternatives spectrum, from established platforms to first-time, emerging, and diverse managers raising institutional money for the first time.

What we do

The right allocators are already looking. We find them.

Many institutions run dedicated programs to back new, upcoming, and diverse managers. Many more hold active mandates in specific asset classes. STING matches your fund to the ones whose requirements you actually meet, then hands you the shortlist as a report.

Across the alternatives spectrum.

Hedge funds, private equity, venture capital, private credit, and real-asset managers all share one problem: the right institutional capital is hard to find, and harder to qualify.

Whether you're a multi-billion-dollar platform opening a new vehicle or a first-time manager raising an inaugural fund, the model works the same way. It finds the investors and programs whose requirements your fund actually meets.
