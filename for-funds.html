For Funds

Who we work with.

We work with funds across the alternatives spectrum — from established platforms opening new vehicles to first-time, emerging, and diverse managers raising institutional capital for the first time.

What we do

The right allocators are already looking.

Many institutions run dedicated programs for new, emerging, and diverse managers. Many more hold active mandates in specific asset classes. STING identifies the ones whose requirements your fund meets and delivers them as a report.

Established platforms and first funds.

Hedge funds, private equity, venture capital, private credit, and real-asset managers share the same problem: the right institutional capital is hard to find, and harder to qualify.

Whether you are a multi-billion-dollar platform opening a new vehicle or a first-time manager raising an inaugural fund, the screen works the same way. It finds the investors and programs whose requirements your fund meets.
