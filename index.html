Institutional Capital Introduction

The institutional investors your fund actually fits.

We research the institutional investor universe for fund managers raising capital. The model evaluates every possible pairing, rejects more than 90%, and delivers the short list that remains — with the rationale behind every match.

Request an engagement How the model works

Most target lists are directories. Ours is a screen.

Most funds go to market with lists assembled from databases and conference rosters: hundreds of names, most of which were never going to allocate to their strategy, stage, or structure.

We work the other way around. The model starts from the full institutional universe — pensions, endowments, foundations, funds of funds, OCIOs, sovereign and family capital — and eliminates every institution whose mandates and programs your fund does not fit. What remains is the list worth your team's time.

The discipline

90%+

The model rejects more than ninety percent of possible pairings on mandate fit, program eligibility, and asset-class alignment. What survives is a short list of institutions your fund genuinely fits.
