Privacy

Privacy policy.

How we handle the information you share with us through this website.

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Information we collect

The only personal information this site collects is what you submit through the inquiry form or send us by email: your name, firm or fund name, email address, and the details you choose to share about your fund and raise. This site does not use advertising trackers, analytics scripts, or third-party cookies.

How we use it

We use the information you submit solely to evaluate your inquiry, respond to it, and, if we proceed together, scope and deliver an engagement. We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own use.

Service providers

Form submissions are delivered to us by Web3Forms, a form-processing service, and handled by our email provider. These providers process your submission only to deliver it to us.

Confidentiality of engagement details

Details of your strategy and raise shared with us during an inquiry or engagement are treated as confidential and are not shared, shopped, or reused.

Retention and deletion

We keep inquiry correspondence for as long as needed to handle the inquiry or engagement and to meet our legal obligations. To ask what we hold about you or to request deletion, email owen@stingcappartners.com.

Changes

If we change this policy, we will update this page and its date. Questions are welcome at the address above.
