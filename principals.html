Why founder-led matters.

At most capital-introduction firms, your raise is handed to whoever has capacity. At STING there is no handoff. The principals run every engagement themselves: the scoping call, the model run, the curation, the delivery.

That is a deliberate constraint. It caps how many engagements the firm takes on, and it means the people accountable for your shortlist are the same people who built the system that produced it.
