Why founder-led matters.

At larger capital-introduction firms, an engagement is assigned to whoever has capacity. At STING there is no handoff. The founders run every engagement themselves: the scoping call, the model run, the curation, and the delivery.

That constraint caps how many engagements the firm takes on at once. It also means the people accountable for your shortlist are the people who built the system that produced it.
